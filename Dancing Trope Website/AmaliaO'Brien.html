Short Biography

Amalia O'Brien - a young and ambitious dancer coming from North Ireland, finding her roots in more traditional dance, everything changed for her when she discovered modern world music. Having a whole new view on dance she travelled to Australia to rediscover herself and her dance and when she met Aqib, and the rest is history.
