Short Biography

Aqib Cameron - from a young age always wanted to express his views to others. He started by drawing his thoughts and showing it to them to friends and relatives. However when his uncle from France came over, and with him, he bought a small cd with a video of a dance troupe, Aqib instantly fell in love with dancing and from then on, he too started to dance.
